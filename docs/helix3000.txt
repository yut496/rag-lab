The Helix 3000 is a home espresso machine designed for enthusiasts.
It uses a 15-bar pressure pump to extract espresso.
The water tank holds 1.8 liters and is removable for easy refilling.
A built-in conical burr grinder offers 40 grind settings.
The machine weighs 6.2 kilograms and is available in matte black and brushed steel.
It comes with a 2-year limited warranty.
The Helix 3000 reaches brewing temperature in about 25 seconds.
